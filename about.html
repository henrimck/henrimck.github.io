About Frank Fact Generator

Our Story

Our founder and sole developer, Henri, believes that lying isn’t always wrong. In fact, he thinks it can be fun. He believes that convincing people that a lie is true is morally neutral, as long as the lie is lighthearted and the person telling it comes clean.

Explore Our Features

  • Fact Generation: Leave the topic blank and click “Generate Fact” for a random lie.
  • Fact About: Enter a topic and click “Generate Fact” to get a lie tailored to it.
  • Fact Saving: Save and star your favorite lies in your profile (coming back soon).

Why Choose Us?

This is the only app that helps you generate convincing, ridiculous lies just for fun. Whether you're trying to make your friends laugh, practice persuasion, or just need a new icebreaker, Frank Fact Generator has you covered.

Our Brand

We acknowledge that lying can be harmful — which is why our facts are designed to be lighthearted, silly, and non-offensive. This project is about play, not harm. Everything is built with emotional intelligence and a commitment to fun, inclusive humor.
