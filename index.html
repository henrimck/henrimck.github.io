      Our Story

      Our founder and sole developer, Henri, believes that lying isn’t always wrong. In fact, he thinks it can be fun. He believes that convincing people that a lie is true is morally neutral, as long as the lie is lighthearted and the person telling it comes clean.

      Explore Our Features

      Fact Generation

      Simply leave the topic blank on the homepage and press “Generate Fact” to obtain a random fact.

      Fact About

      Input any topic on the homepage and press “Generate Fact” to find a specific fact.

      Fact Saving

      If you have a favorite fact, navigate to your profile. You’ll find your past facts and stars next to them. Mark a favorite fact by pressing the star icon.

      Why Choose Us?

      We’re not sure how many apps there are, but we know there are many. However, do you know how many apps generate untrue fun facts while helping users improve their speaking, persuading, and debating skills in a fun and comfortable setting? Just one: ours. So if you enjoy presenting lies as fun facts but don’t want to fabricate them from scratch, choose us. Additionally, our app relieves the pain of not having a lie to tell, not having the time to craft a lie worth telling, or the pain of having writer’s block.

      Our Brand

      We acknowledge that lying can be detrimental, which is why all generations are inherently lighthearted.
